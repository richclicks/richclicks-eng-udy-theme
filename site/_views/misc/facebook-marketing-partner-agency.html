RichClicks is part of the Facebook Marketing Partners for Agencies hub, a programme structured specifically for agencies that manage advertising campaigns on behalf of brands or other companies. It's built to support agencies, grow expertise and know-how and help our clients succeed through specific insights, best practice, training and much more.

The marketing programme offers a wide range of benefits, including tools, resources, training and support, enabling us to provide an excellent service to our clients.

Facebook Marketing Partners for Agencies: benefits to support agencies and brands

The Facebook Marketing Partner label is Facebook's most prestigious partnership level awarded to the best companies in the industry, given to only a few players in Europe, and allows RichClicks to offer advertisers and agencies a more advanced advisory service on the Facebook ecosystem through privileged access to tools, resources and training offered by the platform.

Some of the key resources include:

Support 

Facebook Marketing Partner agencies can enjoy a direct relationship with Facebook and request in-depth consultations with members of their support team, who are willing to help with very complex projects at any time. So, should any issues of any kind arise with one of our clients, we enjoy a priority troubleshooting relationship over agencies not carrying the Facebook Marketing Partner badge. As Facebook is inundated with issues and support requests on a daily basis, this can ensure that our managed accounts are backed up and running in hours rather than days/weeks.

Knowledge

Being an official partner allows our team to receive one-on-one training with Facebook and be the first to learn about new features and media products. This allows us to use the latest methods and tools to generate added value for our clients.

Events

Access to all closed Facebook events. This allows us to be among the first to know about updates and optimisations to the advertising platform in order to develop highly personalised strategies for each type of client/project.
